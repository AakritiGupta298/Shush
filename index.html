About Us

Shush is a platform to facilitate conversation about experiences that are unique to women across health and hygiene that are often "shushed" or dropped out of conversation. Our platform is inundated with well researched facts about the major feminine health concerns and also provides a conduit for women to freely discuss and explore the nuances of their womanhood by means of Shush blog (Shhtories) and chatroom (Shhelp). Learning is boring no more!!
